Our Values

  • Empower students with knowledge and skills necessary for success in their chosen fields.
  • Foster a culture of lifelong learning and professional development.
  • Contribute to the socio-economic development of the region through education and research.

  • Bachelor of Business Administration
  • Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering

Additionally, we offer various postgraduate programs, diploma courses, and certificate programs in different fields of study.
